Philosophy

Vision

Every film begins as something unseen. I chase the images that won't leave me alone - the ones that demand to exist on screen.

Truth

The camera doesn't lie, but it can reveal. I'm drawn to stories that expose what people carry beneath the surface.

Tension

Great cinema lives in the space between frames. I build worlds where silence speaks as loudly as dialogue.

Craft

Every cut, every grade, every sound design choice is intentional. The details are the difference between watching and feeling.
